Additional Information

General Description

Avocado's all natural latex hybrid lineup currently consists of the following mattresses for adults:

  • Avocado Green — Available in Standard, Pillow Top, and Box Top.
  • Luxury Organic — Certified organic in its entirety. Available in Standard, Pillow Top, and Box Top.
  • Eco Organic — Certified organic in its entirety.

All of them feature:

  • Organix Latex — Our Dunlop latex is GOLS-certified organic (CU863637) and FSC certified, sourced from farms and facilities in India and Guatemala. It's made with 95 percent or more organic material.
  • Organic Wool — Our GOTS-certified organic wool (CU863637) is sourced from herds in the Himalayas. It's a renewable resource with exceptional temperature regulation and inherent fire resistance properties. 
  • Organic Cotton — We use GOTS-certified organic cotton (CU863637) ticking and an elegant organic cotton canvas on the base, sourced from India, Turkey, Canada, and North Carolina.
  • Coils Made With Recycled Steel — Made in our factory from double-tempered recycled steel wire in two premium gauges for improved durability, with a reinforced header and side rails for proper edge support.

Warranty

  • Length: 25-year warranty.
  • Indentation coverage: 1.5" and deeper.
  • Proration: 10 years of full ("non-prorated") replacement coverage + 15 years of partial ("prorated") replacement coverage.
  • Other Notes: Indentation coverage is 1.5" for all Avocado mattress models except the Luxury Plush, which is 2.0"..
