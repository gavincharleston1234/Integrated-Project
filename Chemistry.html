Reduce, Reuse, Recycle

We all live thanks to Earth's resources. Increased manufacturing decreases Earth's finite resources and creates more waste.
Recycling reduces the use of limited resources, saves fuel and energy, and reduces landfill use.

Recycling Polymers

Separating different types of polymers is difficult and expensive. Low levels of recycling are due to challenges in polymer separation.

Recycling Metals

Metals are extracted from minerals in ores.
Using up metal ores leads to more quarries, destroying habitats and causing pollution. Recycling metals saves resources and energy, and is rather easy once separated from other materials.

Recycling Glass

Glass is easily recyclable by crushing and melting it after color separation. Making recycled glass requires less energy, reducing fossil fuel use, even though raw materials for new glass are cheap.
