What is avocado mattress?

Avocado is a Certified B Corporation and carbon-negative, meaning they offset more carbon emissions than they produce through renewable energy and reforestation projects. Their mattresses are durable and long-lasting, reducing waste, and they offer vegan options for those seeking cruelty-free products. With a commitment to sustainability and ethical sourcing, Avocado helps consumers live more sustainably while maintaining comfort and well-being.

Is Avocado Green Mattress certified?

The Avocado Eco Organic Mattress and other beds from Avocado, including the Eco Green, Vegan and Luxury Organic, are all certified organic with GOLS-certified latex and GOTS-certified cotton.
